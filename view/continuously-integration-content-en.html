Continuously Integration

Mistakes in work processes are something that cannot be denied at any company, eventhough in a stable company unmindful of potential mistakes might also be happened. This includes the small things that are ignored by employees that can be a problem during the work processes of the company. This makes Pojokan.Io reflect and learn for these things, in this company we implement a work system called Continuously Integration.

Continuously Integration is a work system that requires all parts of the company to always integrate each other works. This allows us to always check the ongoing progress to avoid out of scopes work. Although this system alone cannot completely free us from all kinds of potential problems can occur, however with this we can more easily find and solve existing problems.

The purpose of implementing this work system itself are:

  1. Minimum Time Cost

    The process of integration itself is basically certainly applied in any job, but what distinguishes this working system itself lies in the word "Continuously", which means "Sustainable". By applying this one simple thing principle of continuous integration process, we can save time in dealing with existing problems.

  2. Early Find Mistake

    The integration process that is carried out on an ongoing basis automatically will require us to check the work progress all the time. In long time span of this implementation, we tend to discover problem during the integration process. Therefore the entire process verificvation is carried out periodically and continuously allows us to be able to find potential problems early.

  3. Rapid Delivery

    Quick response to problems and their earlier discoveries can make developers more focused to develop our products, therefore it can be launched more quickly.
